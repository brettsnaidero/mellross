Home and Land Packages

Many people often talk of the burden of having to research and hunt to find a block of land, only then to still be faced with having to search around for the right house design for them (and to suit the block). For our clients who feel this way we are striving to make the whole process a whole lot easier. With a Mellross Homes Home and Land package you tell us what you’re looking for, so that we can put our knowledge and experience to work to help you find the home that’s going to be perfect for you and your family. At Mellross Homes we offer a great range of Pre-Determined Home and Land Packages to suit anyone from the first home buyer, the investor or family living. We can Also tailor make a package to suit you!
